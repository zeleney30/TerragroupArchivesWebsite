What is Terragroup Archives?

Terragroup Archives is the all in one unofficial Escape from Tarkov helper. Our desktop application provides multiple tools to assist players such as Interactive Maps, Ammo Charts, Quest Helper, and Real Time Price Checker, accessible through our built in game overlay. We also include a custom generated Loadout Creator, which is shareable between friends.

Interactive Maps

With our Interactive Maps users have the ability to pan and zoom as they wish, while also choosing what information to display for each map.

Ammo Charts

With our Ammo Charts users can see how different ammo types and subtypes compare to eachother in a visual way. Users can also see how specific ammos will effect specific armors.

Quest Helper

Our Quest Helper system users will be able to track exactly what tasks they have completed and their progress for each task. Users can also see future tasks and rewards.

Real Time Price Checker

Our Real Time Price Checker is a one of a kind system which displays an overlay of the current flea market price whenever you hover over an item. No more switching between your inventory and the flea market to check prices.

Loadout Generator

The Loadout Generator will create a custom loadout for your PMC based on some restrictions that you set. The generator will choose every item in your loadout and are easily shareable between friends.

In Game Overlay

Interact with the Terragroup Archives features directly in game without having to switch tabs or use multiple programs. Our application integrates seamlessly into the game for a hassle free user experience.
